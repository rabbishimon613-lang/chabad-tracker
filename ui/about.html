What the colors mean

  • RED at least one conviction or indictment among affiliated personnel
  • ORANGE charges filed or civil settlement
  • YELLOW open investigation or credible allegation
  • CLEAN nothing in the corpus (hidden by default — toggle the CLEAN chip on the map legend to show)

Sourcing

Every incident in the dossier shows its source URLs. Incidents resting on a single source carry a SINGLE-SRC badge.

Verifier & quarantine

A 12-layer automated Verifier checks every incident: URL liveness, name-on-page, verbatim quote, source-class weighting, perpetrator-only doctrine, and more. Failures don't get deleted — they surface in the live activity feed below with the Quarantined filter, plain-English reason and all. That feed is the integrity proof of the dataset.

The bureau · live

This is the autonomous research office. Each station polls snapshot.json every few minutes; the mindbox below each character shows the most recent activity. The work it represents is real. Pixel-art layer comes later — for now it's the floor plan and the log lines.
